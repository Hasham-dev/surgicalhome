About Surgical Home

In The name of ALLAH the Compassionate the Merciful.

"We are the basic manufacturers of instruments displayed in the web catalog. The word "Trading" part of company's name may, therefore, not be considered similar to the business of trading enterprises in general."

We are working in the field of Surgical Instruments since 2015, manufacturing and supplying dependable quality instruments to other companies for supply abroad. Our market target is worldwide. We have customers in USA, Spain, Brazil, Australia, Germany etc., thus practically covering almost all the regions of the world. For this purpose Surgical Home is already certifies by FDA under GMP (Goods Manufacturing Practices) regulations which is our standard policy for compliance.

Business Name: Surgical Home

Associated Business Organizations: A- Sialkot Chamber of Commerce & Industry (SCCI), B- Trade Development Authority of Pakistan (TDAP) C- Surgical Instruments Manufacturers Association

Production: Our Major Products Manufacturing and Exported are Surgical, Dental, Veterinary and Beauty Instruments as well as scissors of all sort. We also manage production and supply according to graphical designs & samples, provided by the customers. We continue our technical modifications and improvements of the products. Sound packing is done, keeping in mind the rigors of transportation. We manage the shipment By Sea as well as By Courier.
